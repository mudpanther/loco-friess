Our Philosophy

A Culinary Journey Through Ecuador

At Loco Fries, we honor the rich culinary traditions of Ecuador while embracing contemporary techniques that elevate each dish to an art form.

Passion Refined Through Excellence

Located in the heart of Spring Valley, our intimate establishment reimagines Ecuadorian street food classics through the lens of fine dining. Every dish tells a story of heritage, crafted with locally-sourced ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations.

Our culinary team, led by visionary chefs with deep roots in Ecuadorian cuisine, brings decades of combined experience from renowned kitchens across Latin America and beyond. We source the finest ingredients, from sustainable seafood to heritage produce, ensuring each plate reflects our unwavering commitment to quality.

Whether you're experiencing our signature Salchi preparations or exploring our refined Cevichochos, each visit promises an unforgettable journey through the vibrant flavors of Ecuador, executed with precision and presented with elegance.
