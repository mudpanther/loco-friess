        Our Story        

Where Ecuadorian Tradition Meets Street Food Culture

       
     
     
       
         

¡Sabor que te vuelve loco!

         

Located at 240 N Main St, Spring Valley, NY, LOCO FRIES offers an eclectic fusion of flavors that promises to excite your palate — from mouthwatering fries to succulent shrimp and authentic empanadas.

         

Guests like Jazmin Acevedo and Connie Zhen rave about generous portions and tasty options. Our friendly team — shoutout to Diana — is dedicated to making your visit awesome.

         

Whether you love Ecuadorian cuisine or just want a delightful meal, LOCO FRIES brings a taste of home and a vibrant atmosphere that’ll keep you coming back.
